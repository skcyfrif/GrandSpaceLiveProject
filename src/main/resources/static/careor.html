Construction Manager

Grade:Construction Manager

Location: Bhubaneswar

Reports to:Construction Manager

Qualification:Bachelor’s degree (or equivalent), information civil, or engineering

Job Type: Full-time

Required Skills:

Fresher Key Responsibilities

  • Work closely with the Contracts Manager to plan the project effectively, by ensuring that the project requirements, specification, design drawings, health and safety proposals are all read, understood and agreed, and that a project programme is in place.
  • Set up an emergency plan and general site facilities for the project, ensuring that these are in line with the health and safety proposal.
  • Form and maintain the storage facilities and manage stock planning so that stock, materials, plant and tools are organised to meet the timescales for the project.
  • Troubleshooting: Identify, debug, and resolve technical issues in a timely manner.
  • Form a secure site, including access and egress.
  • Formulate a material schedule for the project, including take off from issued drawings and specifications, and produce requisitions as far ahead of programme periods as possible to enable best prices to be obtained by the Purchasing Department.
  • Produce a labour schedule in conjunction with line manager and project surveyor to ensure sub contractors packages are placed in time to meet the programme. Maintain the schedule and track progress, keeping records on file and copying weekly records to line manager/office..

total work: 1 year (Preferred)
